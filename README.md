## 🏠 Dashboard Overview

After login, you will see:

* Employee Management
* Leave Requests
* Attendance System
* Add Employee Form

---

## 👩‍💼 Manage Employees

### ➕ Add Employee

1. Fill in:

   * Name
   * Department
   * Birth Date
   * Address
   * Salary
2. Click **Add Employee**
3. System will generate:

   * Username
   * Password

👉 Share these credentials with the employee

---

### 👁️ View Employee

* Click **View** to see:

  * Username & Password
  * Department
  * Payslip

---

### ✏️ Edit Employee

* Click **Edit**
* Update details
* Click **Update**

---

### ❌ Delete Employee

* Click **Delete**
* Confirm deletion

---

## 📄 Leave Management

* Employees can apply for leave
* HR can:

  ✔ Approve
  ✔ Reject

👉 Status updates are sent as notifications to employees

---

## 📅 Attendance System

### Mark Attendance

1. Select date
2. Click:

   * ✅ Present
   * ❌ Absent

👉 You can mark attendance for any date

---

### View Attendance Page

* Go to **Attendance section**
* You can:

  * View all records
  * Filter by employee
  * Filter by date range

---

### 📊 Attendance Report

* See:

  * Total Present
  * Total Absent

---

## 🔍 Search Employees

* Use search bar to find employees quickly by name

---

## 🔔 Notifications

* Employees receive notifications when:

  * Leave is approved
  * Leave is rejected

---

## 💡 Notes

* All data is stored locally (browser storage)
* Refresh will NOT delete data
* HR controls the entire system

---

## 🚀 Tips

* Always create employee accounts before login
* Share credentials securely
* Use filters for quick data access

---

## 📌 Summary

This system allows HR to:

✔ Manage employees
✔ Handle leaves
✔ Track attendance
✔ View reports
